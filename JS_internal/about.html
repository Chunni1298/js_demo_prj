Who We are




Vision Mission
    We encourage emerging and established filmmakers with all kinds of backgrounds in their art projects. We support their films day in and day out, presenting them to like-minded professionals and a large audience. Together we increase the creative space for film citizenship and celebrate the different forms of film.
    As an international film festival, we support the power and impact of independent cinema and filmmakers. We stimulate, train, finance, distribute and support filmmakers. By organizing a festival we try to create a stage for independent directors to show their work to a wide and varied audience.
